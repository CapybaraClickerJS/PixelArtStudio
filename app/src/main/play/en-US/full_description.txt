PixelArtStudio is a lightweight, offline-first pixel art editor designed for Android. Create beautiful pixel art directly on your device with a clean, intuitive interface.

Features:
• Paint with pencil, eraser, bucket fill, and color picker tools
• Undo/redo functionality
• Zoom in and out
• Import and export PNG images
• Auto-save your work
• Works offline
• Dark mode interface
• Works on any Android device (7.0+)

Perfect for creating sprites, icons, small animations, and more. All your work is saved locally on your device.
